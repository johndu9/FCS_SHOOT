Game Controls (CHANGEABLE IN CONTROLS.TXT):
  0 to mute sound.
  - to lower volume.
  = to increase volume.
  Enter at score screen to play again.
  Backspace at score screen to go to main menu.
  Esc to quit the game.
  F3 to toggle debug mode.
  F4 to toggle frame cap.
  F5 to toggle text.
  F6 to toggle player number.
  F7 to toggle trails.
  F10 to toggle pause.
  F11 to toggle fullscreen.
