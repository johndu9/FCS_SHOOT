SPACECRAFTIA:
  Ruled over by the benevolent GLORIOUS LEADER
  for countless decades, SPACECRAFTIA is a
  prosperous league of planets numbering at
  nearly 10,000 members. Its fleet is massive,
  and is considered a threat by all who oppose
  this great nation, for not only does it
  number in the trillions but also utilizes the
  some of the most advanced weapons available.
